About Us

Including all our activities

WHO WE ARE

As stated earlier, the Foundation runs a functional Orphanage home. Currently we are accommodating over fifteen (15) children as well as a teenager, her child and an elderly man whose health the foundation is overseeing.

we envision a community of healthy young men and women living godly and positively, driving towards fulfillment of purpose and whose way of life is bringing transformation to their society.

Part of the Humanitarian services the Foundation has engaged in over the last few years include, distribution of welfare materials to widows and less privileged, educational scholarships and grants, medical support, etc. Almost all the children in the orphanage are in school under educational scholarship by the foundation.

As stated earlier, the Foundation runs a functional Skill Acquisition Center which supports young women by teaching them fashion designing skills and empowers them upon graduation to establish their own tailoring shops
